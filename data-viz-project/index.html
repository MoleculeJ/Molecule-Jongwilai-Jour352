Women and Olympic Gender Gap

By Molecule Jongwilai

November 22, 2025

The underrepresentation of female athletes in the Olympic Games is a topic that has been discussed widely for a long time. This brief article roughly explored the data of over 270,000 individual Olympic athletes competing in individual events, aiming to shed light on how women are recognized compared to men in the world's largest sports event, and how this situation has progressed over time.
